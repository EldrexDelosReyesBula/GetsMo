# GetsMo

**GetsMo** is a smart, beginner-friendly logic assistant designed to help learners understand and apply the **Rules of Replacement** in propositional logic.

It focuses on one clear goal: to guide users in proving that two propositions are **logically equivalent** through structured, step-by-step reasoning.

---

## About GetsMo

GetsMo is built around the idea that logic should not feel overwhelming.

For many students, topics like equivalence, transformations, and symbolic reasoning can quickly become confusing, especially when multiple rules are involved. GetsMo simplifies this process by turning complex expressions into a guided and understandable experience.

Instead of relying on memorization alone, GetsMo encourages **active learning**. It helps users see how each transformation works, why it is valid, and how different rules connect with one another.

---

## Purpose

GetsMo exists to:

* Make propositional logic easier to understand
* Support beginners in learning Rules of Replacement
* Provide a structured way to prove logical equivalence
* Bridge the gap between theory and actual problem solving

It is not just about getting the correct answer, but about understanding the **process behind the answer**.

---

## Learning Approach

GetsMo follows a step-by-step reasoning model.

Each transformation is based on established logical rules, allowing users to observe how one expression evolves into another. This approach helps build intuition and confidence when working with logical statements.

The system is designed to support both:

* Learners who need guidance
* Users who prefer solving problems on their own

---

## Philosophy

GetsMo is guided by three core ideas:

### 1. Clarity over complexity

Logic should be presented in a way that is easy to follow and understand.

### 2. Learning by doing

Users learn best when they actively engage with problems, not just observe solutions.

### 3. Consistency in reasoning

Every step must be logically valid and based on recognized rules.

---

## Who It’s For

GetsMo is designed for:

* Students studying propositional logic
* Beginners learning Rules of Replacement
* Anyone who wants to practice logical reasoning
* Self-learners who prefer structured guidance

---

## Core Concept

At its core, GetsMo works with expressions in the form:

```
Proposition A = Proposition B
```

The goal is to determine whether both sides are logically equivalent by applying valid transformations.

---

## Why GetsMo

Learning logic can feel abstract, especially when dealing with symbols and multiple rules at once. GetsMo provides a more approachable way to interact with these concepts by focusing on clarity, structure, and understanding.

It is not just a tool for solving problems, but a space for learning how logical reasoning works.

---

## Summary

GetsMo is more than a logic assistant.
It is a learning environment built to make propositional logic clearer, more intuitive, and more accessible for everyone.
